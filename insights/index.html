The insights...

  • Embrace your idea, no matter how abstract it is.

  • Define what you want to achieve, what is this for?

  • See the contrast between ideas.

  • Capture those important bits of information.

  • Recognize patterns of information.

  • Do not forget the context of your design.

  • Things will change and fail along the process, do not panic!

  • Analyze and synthesize information.

See the small and big stories behind.

Try different perspectives.

Expect the unexpected, and learn from it.

Do not get stuck on mistakes, follow them.

Do not underestimate the small details.

And remember, it is not only another project; the work is part of you.
